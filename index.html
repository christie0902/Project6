Mark Hamill

Mark Richard Hamill was born in Oakland, California, on September 25, 1951, the fourth of seven children. His father was a captain in the United States Navy, so the large family constantly moved from place to place. Mark spent his childhood alternately in California, Virginia, New York and also in Japan, where he graduated from Yokohama's High School. He then went to Los Angeles to study theater acting.
